Eduacation in New York City

Education is a great opportunity to quickly assimilate the change from one country to another as it includes learning a new language, in this case the English language. Besides, it includes the learning about the new country's culture, laws and its history.

New York City has the largest public education system in the United States. Any child between the ages of 5 and 18 has the right to study regardless of their immigration status. The schools your child is assigned to depends on her address, as they are assigned schools located within the zone.

Robert Seaman Elementary School is a top rated, public school located in JERICHO, NY. It has 292 students in grades K-5 with a student-teacher ratio of 11 to 1. According to state test scores, 92% of students are at least proficient in math and 92% in reading

Best High School in NYC

Stuyvesant High School

Stuyvesant High School is the number one of 520 high schools within New York. Students have the opportunity to take advanced placement courseworks and exams. The total minority enrollment is 81%, and 43% of students are economically disadvantaged.

Bronx High School of Science

Bronx High School of Science is a top rated, public school located in BRONX, NY. It has 2,983 students in grades 9-12 with a student-teacher ratio of 21 to 1.Ninety percent of their students are at least proficient in math and 99% in reading.
Staten Island Technical High School

Staten Island Technical High School is a top rated, public school located in STATEN ISLAND, NY. It has 1,334 students in grades 9-12 with a student-teacher ratio of 20 to 1. Ninety percent of students are at least proficient in math and reading.

Best Univesities for adult education

The City University of New York (CUNY)

CUNY is the largest urban university system in the United States, comprising 25 campuses: eleven senior colleges, seven community colleges, one undergraduate honors college, and seven postgraduate institutions. CUNY has served a diverse student body, especially those excluded from or unable to afford private universities.

State University of New York

SUNY is a system of public colleges and universities in New York State. It is the largest comprehensive system of universities, colleges, and community colleges in the United States. This univesity provides lower-cost college-level education and adult education for residents of the state as the state of New York assists in financing its system.

Pell Grant

Federal Pell Grants are awarded only to undergraduate students who have not yet been able to earned a bachelor's or a professional degree. Students who financial need must fill out a Free Application for Federal Student Aid(FAFSA) to be elegible.

The Tuition Assistance Program (TAP)

TAP, New York's largest grant program, helps eligible New York residents who attend state postsecondary institutions pay tuition. TAP grants are based on the New York State taxable income of the applicant and the applicant's family.
