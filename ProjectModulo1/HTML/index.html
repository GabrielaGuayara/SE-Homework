New York Guide

The Big Apple is a guide which is aimed at migrants who have just arrived in New York City.This guide is aimed at helping migrants settle in this new city and share information on basic services that are necessary for the lives of New Yorkers The information that you will found in this guide is basic but essential for daily life.

How is living in NYC?

01 Cultural diversity everywhere

The city of New York throughout the years has been the destination of many migrants, it is known that 37% of the population has been born in another country. Therefore, you can meet people of different nationality and culture every day.

02 Goodbye homemade food, hello fast food!

Busy work schedules can cause you to not have time to cook something to eat so fast food is the best option. In NYC, restaurants are full every day and you can find lines of people waiting to buy from food carts.

03 Traffic and noise in the streets

Traffic is common in the city of NY so the noise of the whistles of trucks and cars can be annoying. Hearing the sound of ambulances in the middle of the night is common, so you must get used to the noise of this great city.
