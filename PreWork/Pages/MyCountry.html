About my country

Ecuador is a multi-ethnic and multi-cultural country. It is located at the top of South America, between Peru and Colombia. Is capital is Quito that is known for its intact Spanish colonial center. Moreover, My country is known as one of the most biodiverse countries in the world. Many tourists visit Ecuador in order to discover its exceptional flora and fauna.

Geography

Ecuador is divided into four regions:The Coast, Andes Mountains, Amazon region and Galapagos Islands. My country is made up of 24 provinces. Ten provinces make up La Sierra or Andes Montains, seven provinces make up La Costa or The Cost and Las Islas Galapagos or Galapagos Islands makes up their own province.
